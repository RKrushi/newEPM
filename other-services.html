Common Questions

Frequently asked

Everything you need to know about our advisory services.

What is estate planning and why do I need it?
Estate planning is the process of arranging for the management and disposal of your estate during your lifetime and after death. It ensures your assets reach the right people, minimises estate taxes, and avoids costly legal disputes. Without a plan, your estate is distributed by law — which may not reflect your wishes.
How much can I save through tax planning?
The savings depend on your income bracket and current tax structure. Most clients save between ₹50,000 and ₹3,00,000 annually through strategic use of deductions under 80C, 80D, 80CCD, HRA optimisation, and capital gains tax management. Our advisors identify every legal avenue available to you.
What goals can EPM Wealth help me plan for?
We plan for every major life goal — children's education, marriage, home purchase, retirement corpus, business exit, world travel, and even philanthropic goals. We quantify each goal, calculate the required corpus, and build a time-bound investment plan to achieve it with high probability.
Is EPM Wealth's advice independent and unbiased?
Yes, absolutely. EPM Wealth is a SEBI-registered Investment Advisor (RIA). As an RIA, we are legally required to act in your best interest and are not permitted to receive commissions from product providers. Our only compensation is the fee you pay us — ensuring completely unbiased advice.
How do I get started?
Simply click "Let's Talk" and fill in our contact form. An EPM Wealth advisor will reach out within 24 hours to schedule a discovery call. The initial consultation is completely free and carries no obligation. We believe in earning your trust before asking for your business.
